# PairSlash Phase 3.5 Evidence Table

Use this table as the source of truth for all synthesis claims.
Every cluster, contradiction, and ranked opportunity must trace back to one or
more evidence rows here.

## Signal Type Rules

- `behavioral_pain`: direct description of what happened.
- `workaround_cost`: time, rework, manual checking, or cognitive load.
- `trust_threshold`: what must be true before reuse.
- `repeat_use_signal`: why they would or would not come back next week.
- `install_friction`: setup, doctor, or environment friction.
- `governance_need`: auditability, explicit review, traceability, control.
- `praise_only`: positive reaction without pain or behavior change.
- `would_use_claim`: future-tense statement without recurring trigger.
- `contradiction`: explicit tension in answers or between belief and behavior.

## Strength Rules

- `strong`: direct quote or concrete incident with recurrence or cost.
- `medium`: concrete but partial, or repeated without strong quote support.
- `weak`: generic opinion, praise, or speculative statement.

## Exclusion Rules

Do not let these dominate the table:

- praise without a real incident
- "would use" claims without next-week trigger
- abstract requests for autonomy or orchestration
- install complaints with no recurring workflow pain

Keep them if they matter, but tag them as `weak` and do not let them drive
opportunity ranking.
