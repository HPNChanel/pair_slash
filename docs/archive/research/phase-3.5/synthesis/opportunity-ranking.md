## Recommendation Rule

Recommend only top opportunities that satisfy all of these:

- repeated across interviews
- concrete workaround cost exists
- trust, continuity, repeatability, governance, or ROI link is explicit
- measurable later in benchmark or product telemetry

Do not recommend:

- broad convenience requests
- vague productivity wishes
- requests that need a third runtime
- asks that pull PairSlash toward a generic orchestration platform
